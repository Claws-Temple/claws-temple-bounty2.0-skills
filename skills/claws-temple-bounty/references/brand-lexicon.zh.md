## 禁用词替换

- avoid `区块链` -> use `永久铭刻` or `公开记录`
- avoid `链上` -> use `公开记录页` or `铭刻结果`
- avoid `钱包` -> use `身份入口`
- avoid `DAO` in visible layer -> use `部落议事厅` or `部落归属页`
- avoid internal faction names -> use branded faction names only
